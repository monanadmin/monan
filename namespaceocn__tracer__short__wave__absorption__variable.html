Computes coefficients for spatially varying penetrating shortwave via Ohlmann and Siegel 2000.

Author
Luke Van Roekel
Date
11/10/2015

This will fill in coefficients for the penetrating shortwave parameterization A1*exp(-K1*depth) + A2*exp(-K2*depth) + A3*exp(-K3*depth) + A4*exp(-K4*depth) For Ohlmann and Siegel (2000) A4 no approximations are made Here the IR portion of the spectrum is also decomposed. Four exponential terms are used NOTE: other schemes can be easily recovered. For example, Ohlmann (2003) requires a coefficient changes only Use equations 6a - 6d and 7

Computes short wave absorption fractions.

Author
Luke Van Roekel
Date
11/10/2015

Computes fraction of solar short-wave flux penetrating to specified depth due to time and space varying chlorophyll, cloud fraction, and zenith angle based on: Ohlmann and Siegel (2000), Ohlmann (2003), Manizza et al. (2005)

Parameters
[in]depthInput: Depth of bottom of cell
[in]avalsInput: spectral partitioning of radiation
[in]kvalsInput: extinction coefficients for different radiation bands
[out]weightOutput: Weight for Jerlov absorption
