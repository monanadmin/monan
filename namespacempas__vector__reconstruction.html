Detailed Description

MPAS Vector reconstruction module.

Author
Xylar Asay-Davis, Todd Ringler
Date
03/28/13

This module provides routines for performing vector reconstruction from edges to cell centers.

1d MPAS Vector reconstruction routine

Author
Xylar Asay-Davis, Todd Ringler, Matt Hoffman
Date
03/28/13

Purpose: reconstruct vector field at cell centers based on radial basis functions Input: grid meta data and vector component data residing at cell edges Output: reconstructed vector field (measured in X,Y,Z) located at cell centers

Parameters
[in]meshpoolInput: Mesh information
[in]uInput: Velocity field on edges
[out]ureconstructxOutput: X Component of velocity reconstructed to cell centers
[out]ureconstructyOutput: Y Component of velocity reconstructed to cell centers
[out]ureconstructzOutput: Z Component of velocity reconstructed to cell centers
[out]ureconstructzonalOutput: Zonal Component of velocity reconstructed to cell centers
[out]ureconstructmeridionalOutput: Meridional Component of velocity reconstructed to cell centers
[in]includehalosInput: Logical flag that allows reconstructing over halo regions
