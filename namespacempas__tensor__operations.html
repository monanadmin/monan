Detailed Description

MPAS tensor operations.

Author
Mark Petersen
Date
April 2013

This module contains the routines for computing the strain rate tensor, the divergence of a tensor, and a testing routine to verify these work properly.

Convert a 2D tensor to a tensor in R3, at an edge.

Author
Mark Petersen
Date
April 2013

Given a 2D tensor in symetric 3-index form that is rotated such that the 1-direction is towards the edge normal, and the 2-direction is towards the edge tangent, this routine rotates the tensor to R3, and returns an R3 tensor in symetric 6-index form.

Parameters
[in]strainrate2dedgeInput: strain rate tensor at edge, 2D, in symmetric 3-index form
[in]meshpoolInput: mesh information
[in]edgetangentvectorsInput: unit vector tangent to an edge
[in]includehaloInput: If true, halo cells and edges are included in computation
[out]strainrater3edgeOutput: strain rate tensor at edge, R3, in symmetric 6-index form
