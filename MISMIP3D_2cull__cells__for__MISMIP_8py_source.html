2"""
3This script will mark for culling the periodic rows/columns at the top, bottom,
4left, and right of a mesh generated by periodic_hex. It will remove an extra row
5at the top of the mesh in order to make the mesh symmetrical about the E-W axis.
6This results in cleaner solutions for MISMIP.
7
8It creates a new field called cullCell and marks those locations with 1s.
9This mesh can then be run through mesh_conversion_tools/MpasCellCuller.x to
10remove those boundary rows/columns, leaving a non-periodic planar mesh.
11
12The logic to do this is to mark the max and min rows and the two max and min
13columns, so it is specific to a periodic_hex mesh. If it were wished to
14make this tool general, it could calculate cell-to-cell distances between
15all neighboring cells and mark for culling any cell pairs that have
16distances greater than, say, half of the range in x/y values of the entire mesh.
17"""
