updates effective density in land ice

Author
Xylar Asay-Davis
Date
10/03/2015

This routine updates the value of the effective seawater density displaced by land ice, based on Archimedes' principle. The effective density is smoothed and extrapolated by averaging with nearest neighbors (cellsOnCell).
