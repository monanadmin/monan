MPAS-Ocean Vector on a tangent plane.

Author
Todd Ringler
Date
02/19/2014

Given two points measured in (x,y,z) and lying on the unit sphere, find the vector (p_out) that lies on the plane perpendicular to the p_1 vector and points in the direction of the projection of p_2 onto the tangent plane.

NOTE : p_1 and p_2 are assumed to be of unit length NOTE : p_out is normalized to unit length
