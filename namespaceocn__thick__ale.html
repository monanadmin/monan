Computes desired ALE thickness at new time.

Author
Mark Petersen
Date
August 2013

This routine computes the desired Arbitrary Lagrangian-Eulerian (ALE) thickness at the new time. It uses the ALE formulation, and includes contributions from SSH variations (z-star), high-frequency divergence (z-tilde), and imposes a minimum layer thickness.

Parameters
[in]meshpoolInput: horizonal mesh information
[in]verticalmeshpoolInput: vertical mesh information
[in]sshInput: sea surface height
[in]newhighfreqthicknessInput: high frequency thickness. Alters ALE thickness.
[out]ale_thicknessOutput: desired thickness at new time
[out]errOutput: error flag
